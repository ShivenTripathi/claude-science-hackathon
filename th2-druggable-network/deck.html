The idea

STAT6 was just drugged — what else on the inside can be?

STAT6 was recently orally degraded with biologic-like efficacy in atopic dermatitis, proving an intracellular master regulator of the type-2 program is druggable. Most Perturb-seq target work stops at "which knockout shifts the phenotype."

The move here: fuse functional genomics with chemical tractability in one ranking — score every perturbation for how well it phenocopies the STAT6/GATA3 collapse, then ask which of those nodes are (i) off the crowded IL4R–STAT6 / JAK axis, (ii) a real small-molecule or degrader handle, (iii) active only in stimulated cells, and (iv) up in AD patients.

Un-polarized Rest/Stim CD4s — this is a transcriptional-footprint resemblance to the STAT6/GATA3 collapse, not a functional Th2 differentiation assay. Carried throughout.
Why this calibrates where the atlas did not

Anchor on a positive signal, not a noisy residual

A naive "Th2 down & Th1 flat" residual is dominated by low-power noise — FDR ≈ 1 in the sibling projects. GATA3 and STAT6, by contrast, have a measurable trans-footprint, so scoring who else reproduces their collapse is positively anchored. On the informative program genes the two anchors agree at r = +0.63 (vs ~ −0.1 genome-wide). The classifier calibrates:

  • Anchors self-recover — GATA3 #1 in all three conditions (0.95 Rest), STAT6 #2.
  • Held-out IL4R recovers out-of-sample — 0.12 → 0.40 → 0.61 across Rest → Stim8hr → Stim48hr.
  • Permutation null — top scores 0.42–0.49 vs shuffled 95th ≈ 0.04, p < 0.001.
  • Known-regulator recall — median 87th percentile (vs 50th); the naive atlas was recall@250 = 0.

ITK & INPP5D/SHIP1 — proximal TCR-signaling nodes with existing chemical matter and AD/allergy genetics, entirely off the cytokine-receptor axis; both fire only in stimulated cells. KDM8, UBA5, DOHH — under-explored enzyme handles with the strongest patient-lesional upregulation.

Patient anchoring

Candidate expression in AD lesional skin (GSE147424)

Per-candidate lesional-vs-control differential expression in the T-cell (CD3⁺) compartment of an AD skin scRNA-seq cohort. Many candidates are significantly up at the cell level (MWU), including the paper's own validated hit FBXO32.

Pseudobulk (4 lesional vs 11 control) log-fold-changes are directionally positive but mostly do not survive multiple-testing correction — small cohort, sparse T-cell sampling. Supporting evidence of direction, not confirmation.
A coherent under-explored module

UFMylation — a hypothesis, stated honestly

Four UFMylation-pathway members (DDRGK1, UBA5, UFL1, UFM1) phenocopy the collapse and are activation-gated. Patient anchoring is partial: DDRGK1 (+0.70) and UBA5 (+0.83) are up in AD lesional T cells, but UFL1 (−0.01) and UFM1 (−0.08) are flat. The ligase/adaptor arm trends up while the modifier does not — so the module does not co-move coherently in patients.

This is a lead to test, not a validated module — and saying so is the point of a patient-anchored screen.

Caveats — carried, not buried

What this evidence is and is not

  • Un-polarized cells. Transcriptional-footprint resemblance, not a functional type-2 assay.
  • Activation-coupled score. Captures the anchors' whole footprint (heavily TCR-linked); correlates only weakly with cytokine-specific suppression (Spearman −0.14 to −0.19).
  • Nominal patient significance. Directionally positive, mostly not adjusted-significant in this cohort.
  • Tractability is a database call. A handle existing ≠ a feasible degrader.
  • Single-perturbation screen. Mechanism ordering is inferred; the confirming step is a double-perturbation epistasis test against GATA3.
Bottom line

A druggable type-2 network beyond the Dupixent axis

Anchoring on the STAT6/GATA3 collapse turns a mis-calibrated residual into a validated classifier, and layering tractability + genetics + AD-skin expression onto it surfaces ITK and INPP5D/SHIP1 as the most actionable off-axis nodes, with KDM8, UBA5, DOHH as under-explored enzyme handles up in patients.

Next: functional IL-4/5/13 readout in polarized Th2 + a double-perturbation epistasis test against GATA3 for the top off-axis leads.
