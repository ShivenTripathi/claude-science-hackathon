# Mechanistic Dossier: RAB21

**Screen context.** Genome-scale CRISPRi Perturb-seq in primary human CD4+ T cells (Zhu & Dann et al. 2025, Marson lab). RAB21 scores as a candidate *selective* Th2 suppressor: composite 0.818; best th2_arm −0.446 with th1_arm −0.127 at that point; selective in **Rest** and **Stim48hr**; th2_marker_z −1.38, th1_marker_z −0.72, cellcycle_z −0.06, global_mag 1.02; on-target KD z −26.06; cross-donor r 0.852.

> **Framing caveat (applies throughout).** These are Rest/Stim CD4+ T cells, **not** polarized Th1/Th2 cells. "Selective Th2 suppression" here means the knockdown's *transcriptional footprint* resembles selective loss of the Th2 program. This is a phenotypic inference from a single-perturbation screen — **not** a demonstrated mechanism and **not** a functional polarization assay. Every proposed pathway link is a hypothesis to be tested.

## 1. Gene identity & molecular function
RAB21 (RAB21, member RAS oncogene family) encodes an early-endosomal Rab GTPase that cycles between GDP- and GTP-bound states to regulate endosomal membrane trafficking (RefSeq; GeneCards). Its best-established role is in **integrin trafficking**: RAB21 binds the cytoplasmic tails of integrin α-subunits and controls their endo/exocytic traffic, and RAB21 knockdown impairs integrin-mediated cell adhesion and motility [Pellinen 2006]. RAB21-regulated integrin traffic is also required for cytokinesis [Pellinen 2008]. More recently, RAB21 was shown to support **retromer-mediated recycling of the glucose transporter GLUT1/SLC2A1**; RAB21 depletion mis-sorts GLUT1 to lysosomes, reduces glucose uptake, and activates the AMPK–ULK1 pathway and autophagy [Pei 2023]. In macrophages, RAB21 associates with a TRAF3–MKK3 complex and is required for downstream p38 activation and pro-inflammatory cytokine output (IL-1β, TNF-α, IL-17) [Wang 2019]. **No published study links RAB21 to GATA3, STAT6, IL-4, the Th2 cytokine locus, or allergy.** RAB21 is a broadly expressed housekeeping trafficking/metabolic regulator with no known nuclear or transcription-factor activity.

## 2. Selectivity hypothesis (gene → intermediate → Th2, bypassing T-bet/Th1)
Because RAB21 has no direct route to Th2 transcription factors, any effect is necessarily indirect. Two non-exclusive inferred routes:

- **Route A — surface-receptor recycling.** RAB21 governs endo/exocytic traffic of surface receptors (ESTABLISHED for integrins [Pellinen 2006]). Endosomal recycling controls availability of the TCR and signalling receptors at the T-cell surface/immunological synapse (ESTABLISHED that a Rab5–Rab11 recycling axis is required for T-cell activation [Redpath 2019]; note this axis, not RAB21 specifically, is what was shown). *If* RAB21 loss preferentially lowers surface delivery of a Th2-supporting receptor (e.g., IL-4Rα feeding STAT6→GATA3), the Th2 program would fall while the IL-12/STAT4→T-bet Th1 axis — driven by a different receptor and minimally expressed in these unpolarized cells — stays flat (INFERRED; no data connect RAB21 to IL-4Rα).
- **Route B — GLUT1/glycolysis.** RAB21 loss mis-sorts GLUT1 and lowers glucose uptake, activating AMPK [Pei 2023] (ESTABLISHED in cancer cells). Th2 effector differentiation is comparatively glycolysis-dependent, so a metabolic squeeze could preferentially blunt the Th2 program without inducing T-bet (INFERRED; not shown in T cells, and glycolysis restriction is not intrinsically Th2-specific).

The link **RAB21 → GATA3/STAT6/IL-4 program** is INFERRED at every step; no established edge exists.

## 3. Directness call — **INDIRECT**
RAB21 is a cytoplasmic vesicle-trafficking GTPase with no nuclear localization, no DNA binding, and no reported physical or transcriptional interaction with GATA3, STAT6, or the Th2 locus. Any influence on the Th2 program must pass through an intermediate (a recycled receptor, or metabolic/AMPK signalling). Directness is therefore judged indirect with reasonable confidence.

## 4. Artifact scrutiny — **SOME CONCERN**
- **Proliferation collapse:** cellcycle_z −0.06 is essentially flat → **no** evidence of a viability/cell-cycle artifact. Reassuring.
- **Covert Th1-skewer:** th1_marker_z is −0.72 (Th1 markers slightly *down*, not up) → this is **not** a Th1-driving perturbation; it fails the "Th1 goes up" red flag.
- **Global/activation dampening:** this is the real worry. RAB21 is a housekeeping trafficking + metabolic gene, and global_mag 1.02 is elevated. A pattern of "Th2 down, Th1 also mildly down" is exactly what mild global dampening (reduced activation/metabolism) would produce. The score is magnitude-controlled (residual decorrelated from the global axis), and the Th2>Th1 marker margin (−1.38 vs −0.72) tilts toward genuine Th2 preference — but the margin is modest. A general activation/metabolic damp cannot be excluded. On-target KD (z −26) and cross-donor reproducibility (0.852) confirm the effect is real and robust; they do not establish that it is Th2-*selective*.

**Verdict:** the phenotype is reproducible and is not a proliferation-collapse or Th1-flip artifact, but a housekeeping-driven global dampening remains a plausible alternative to true Th2 selectivity.

## 5. Confirming experiment (tests the SELECTIVITY claim)
CRISPRi-knock down RAB21 in naive human CD4+ T cells, then split into Th2-polarizing (anti-CD3/CD28 + IL-4 + anti-IFNγ) and Th1-polarizing (IL-12 + anti-IL-4) cultures. **Readouts:** intracellular IL-4/IL-5/IL-13 and GATA3 (Th2) vs IFNγ/T-bet (Th1) by flow cytometry; CellTrace dilution + viability dye to exclude proliferation/metabolic confounds; surface IL-4Rα and GLUT1 by flow to probe Routes A/B. **Epistasis arm:** co-knock down RAB21 + GATA3 vs each alone. **Expected if hypothesis true:** RAB21 KD lowers IL-4/IL-5/IL-13 and GATA3 under Th2 conditions **without** raising IFNγ/T-bet under Th1 conditions and **without** proliferation/viability loss; RAB21+GATA3 double-KD is no worse than GATA3 alone (RAB21 acting through GATA3). If Th2 and Th1 output fall together with proliferation, the screen signal was global dampening, not selectivity.
